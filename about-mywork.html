My Encountered Adventures While Training To Be A Front End Developer

At the beginning, I was quite confused and bored becaused i had to learn the basic (HTML) before getting into the real business

'The Cascading Style Sheet' (CSS). But while learning the basics, i finally realized that Web Development is like the Phylosophy of life to me. That is to say that, Just like how you code determines the outcome of your website,

that is how any actions or decisions one makes today determines the outcome of ones future .

My Experiences

It was fun when i started learning Css, i really enjoyed targeting items and contents and styling them with my own designs. My favourite was animations . This was because, enabling users to feel engaged in a website is the first priority of every web developer.

My Challenges

It really got to a point when i had an issue with one of the CSS properties known as the "Display" But with the help of my tutor with his consistent exercises and corrections i was able to master the display with a blink of an eye.

And now i am confident to say that i am now an expert in using display properties.
